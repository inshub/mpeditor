## English Version

### 1. Privacy Commitment

mpeditor ("we", "our", or "the Software") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

### 2. Information We Collect

#### 2.1 Local Data Only
mpeditor is a desktop application that runs locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on remote servers.

#### 2.2 Data Stored Locally
- Your articles and editing content are stored locally on your device
- Application settings and preferences are stored locally
- No data is sent to any external servers

#### 2.3 Third-Party Services
If you choose to use AI features or other third-party integrations:
- You explicitly authorize these connections
- Data is sent directly to the respective third-party services
- We do not intermediate or store this data

### 3. No Tracking or Analytics

mpeditor does not include:
- User analytics or tracking
- Usage statistics collection
- Telemetry data
- Crash reporting to external servers

### 4. No Account Required

mpeditor does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the Software.

### 5. Automatic Updates

The Software may check for updates from GitHub Releases. This check only verifies version information and does not transmit any personal data.

### 6. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- You are responsible for backing up your important data
- We cannot access or recover data stored on your device
- Please ensure your device is secure

### 7. Third-Party Links

The Software may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., GitHub). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

### 8. Children's Privacy

mpeditor is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

### 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the Software after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

### 10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please visit: https://github.com/inshub/mpeditor

## 中文版

### 1. 隐私承诺

mpeditor（"我们"、"我们的"或"本软件"）致力于保护您的隐私。本隐私政策说明我们如何处理您的信息。

### 2. 我们收集的信息

#### 2.1 仅限本地数据
mpeditor 是一个在您的设备上本地运行的桌面应用程序。我们不会收集、传输或在任何远程服务器上存储您的任何数据。

#### 2.2 本地存储的数据
- 您的文章和编辑内容本地存储在您的设备上
- 应用程序设置和偏好本地存储
- 不会向任何外部服务器发送数据

#### 2.3 第三方服务
如果您选择使用 AI 功能或其他第三方集成：
- 您明确授权这些连接
- 数据直接发送到相应的第三方服务
- 我们不会中介或存储此数据

### 3. 无跟踪或分析

mpeditor 不包含：
- 用户分析或跟踪
- 使用情况统计收集
- 遥测数据
- 向外部服务器的崩溃报告

### 4. 无需账户

mpeditor 不要求您创建账户或提供任何个人信息即可使用本软件。

### 5. 自动更新

本软件可能会从 GitHub Releases 检查更新。此检查仅验证版本信息，不会传输任何个人数据。

由于所有数据都本地存储在您的设备上：
- 您有责任备份重要数据
- 我们无法访问或恢复存储在您设备上的数据

本软件可能包含指向第三方网站（如 GitHub）的链接。我们不对这些外部网站的隐私做法负责。

### 8. 儿童隐私

mpeditor 不适用于 13 岁以下的儿童。我们不会故意收集儿童的信息。

### 9. 政策变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。更改后继续使用本软件即表示接受更新后的政策。

### 10. 联系我们

如果您对本隐私政策有疑问，请访问：https://github.com/inshub/mpeditor
